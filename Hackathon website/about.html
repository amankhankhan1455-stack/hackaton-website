What is HackSprint?

HackSprint is a community-driven hackathon aimed at promoting innovation, collaboration, and problem-solving. Teams can have 2 to 5 members.

Who should join?

  • Students (undergrad/grad)
  • Developers, Designers, Product enthusiasts
  • Anyone interested in building and learning fast

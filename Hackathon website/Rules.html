Basic Rules

  1. Har team 2-5 members tak ho sakti hai.
  2. All code must be written during the hackathon time window.
  3. Pre-built libraries are allowed — but a complete finished product should not exist before the hackathone.
  4. Respect mentors, volunteers, and fellow participants.
  5. Every team must submit their project before the final submission deadline.

Judging Criteria

  • Innovation & Creativity — 30%
  • Technical Implementation — 30%
  • User Experience & Design — 20%
  • Impact & Feasibility — 20%

Disqualification

Any form of harassment, plagiarism, or rule violation can lead to disqualification. The judges’ decision will be finalS.
